### 有表格如下 TableA，请写出SQL得到结果 TableB

> TableA    

  A | B
  ---|:--:
  1 | 
  2 | 
  3 | 
  5 | 
  6 | 
  7 | 
  9 | 
  10 | 
  11 |


> TableB    

  A | C
  ---|:--:
  1 | 3
  5 | 7
  9 | 11

## SQL 语句：

```
SELECT MIN(C_TABLE.A) AS A, MAX(C_TABLE.A) AS C FROM 
(
	SELECT *, (B_TABLE.A - B_TABLE.ROWS) AS SUB  FROM 
	(
		SELECT A, ROW_NUMBER() OVER(ORDER BY A) AS ROWS FROM A_TABLE
	) B_TABLE
) C_TABLE
GROUP BY C_TABLE.SUB

```
